Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect personal details you provide via our forms or when you contact us, including name, phone number, email, vehicle number, and enquiry details.

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email
  • Vehicle information: registration number and related details
  • Enquiry content related to policies, renewals, or claims

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide quotes, process claims assistance, and share policy updates.

  • To contact you regarding quotes, renewals, and claims support
  • To prepare documentation with insurers and service partners
  • To improve our services and communications

Sharing of information

We may share your details with insurance companies and service partners strictly for the purpose of providing quotes, processing policies, or assisting with claims.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data security & retention

We implement reasonable safeguards and retain information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information where applicable. You can also opt out of non-essential communications.
